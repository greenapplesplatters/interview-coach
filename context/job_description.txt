Software Engineer – Platform & Infrastructure
Apex Systems | Full-Time | Remote (US)

About the Role

We're looking for a Software Engineer to join our Platform team. You'll own the systems that other engineers build on — deployment pipelines, internal tooling, developer experience, and reliability. This is a high-ownership role where you'll work across the stack and directly influence how fast the company can ship.

What You'll Do

- Design, build, and maintain internal developer tooling and deployment automation
- Own reliability work: incident response, SLOs, runbooks, and postmortems
- Build and improve CI/CD pipelines (GitHub Actions, ArgoCD, or similar)
- Work with infrastructure-as-code (Terraform, CDK) to manage cloud resources on AWS
- Collaborate with product and application engineering teams to reduce toil and unblock delivery
- Drive technical decisions and document architecture clearly

What We're Looking For

- 3–6 years of software engineering experience, with at least 2 years in platform, infrastructure, or DevOps adjacent work
- Strong Python or Go experience; comfortable reading other languages
- Hands-on experience with AWS services (ECS, Lambda, RDS, S3, CloudWatch)
- Experience with container orchestration (ECS, EKS, or Kubernetes)
- Comfort with Linux, networking fundamentals, and debugging distributed systems
- Experience with observability tooling (Datadog, Grafana, Prometheus, or similar)
- Strong written communication — you'll write RFCs, runbooks, and postmortems

Nice to Have

- Experience with security tooling or compliance frameworks (SOC 2, HIPAA)
- Contributions to open-source infrastructure tooling
- Background in a high-growth startup environment

Compensation: $145,000 – $185,000 base + equity + benefits
